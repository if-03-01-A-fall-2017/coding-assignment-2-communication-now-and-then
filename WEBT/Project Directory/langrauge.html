    • Language

    Language is a system that consists of the development, acquisition, maintenance and use of complex systems of communication

    Languages evolve and diversify over time, and the history of their evolution can be reconstructed by comparing modern languages to determine which traits their ancestral languages must have had in order for the later developmental stages to occur. A group of languages that descend from a common ancestor is known as a language family.

    Scrips

    Because there are so many diffrent Langauges a lot of diffent scirps exist.

    This makes Communication between people with diffrent Langruages difficult.

    Some example Scripts

    Arabic script

    Arabic does not distinguish between upper and lowercase.

    Arabic is written from right to left and has 28 letters, while European Langruages are written left to right



    Chinese script

    Chinese characters are logograms used in the writing of Chinese.

    In written language, a logogram or logograph is a written character that represents a word or phrase.

    Its written from Left to right, like European Languages

    Chinese has 2 written formats simplified and traditonal, while the traditonal format can still be read and understood,
    but generaly the simplified format is used.
